About the Author

Ammar Alrubayie is an independent researcher, author, and boat designer. He works strictly with scientific evidence to reconstruct suppressed structures of early consciousness and civilization. His research bridges Sumerian, Pharaonic, and other ancient systems through linguistic logic and historical data— without relying on myth, symbolism, or dogma.
